üìä What Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Username (from GitHub/Google)
  • Email address
  • Profile picture (avatar URL)
  • OAuth provider ID
  • Bio (optional, user-provided)

Content You Create

  • Projects (name, description, images, GitHub URL)
  • Issues and bug reports
  • Responses and comments
  • Votes and ratings
  • Uploaded screenshots

Technical Data

  • Session tokens (for authentication)
  • GitHub OAuth token (for API access, encrypted)
  • Account creation timestamp

üéØ How We Use Your Data

We use your data for the following purposes:

  • Authentication: To verify your identity and maintain your session
  • Platform Features: To display your projects, issues, and responses
  • GitHub Integration: To sync issues with GitHub repositories (requires your permission)
  • Leaderboard: To calculate and display your contribution score
  • Communication: To notify you about responses to your issues (if implemented)

‚ö†Ô∏è We do NOT sell your data to third parties or use it for advertising.

ü§ù Data Sharing

Your data may be shared in the following ways:

  • Public Profile: Username, avatar, and bio are visible to other users
  • Public Content: Projects, issues, and responses you create are public
  • GitHub: When syncing issues, data is sent to GitHub's API
  • OAuth Providers: We receive data from GitHub/Google during login

‚úì We do NOT share: Your email address publicly, your OAuth tokens, or any private data with third parties.

üìà Platform Analytics (Xdest)

üü¢ We use Plausible Analytics

Xdest uses Plausible Analytics to understand how our platform is used. Plausible is a privacy-friendly, cookie-free analytics tool:

  • No cookies - No cookie banner required
  • No personal data - We cannot identify individual users
  • No cross-site tracking - Your browsing stays private
  • GDPR compliant - No consent required
  • EU-hosted - Data stays in the European Union

‚Üí Learn more about Plausible's privacy approach

What we track: Page views, referrer sources, country (not city), device type (desktop/mobile), and browser. We use this data to improve Xdest and understand which features are most popular. We do NOT track individual users or build profiles.
